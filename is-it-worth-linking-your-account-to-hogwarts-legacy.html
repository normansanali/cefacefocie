    What are the benefits of linking accounts in Hogwarts Legacy?

    Linking accounts in Hogwarts Legacy offers several benefits. Firstly, you can represent your true Hogwarts house and use your own wand, bringing your Wizarding World identity into the game. You also gain access to an exclusive House Fan-atic School Robe and a Beaked Skull Mask, which are cosmetic items you receive early in the game. Furthermore, achieving certain in-game milestones unlocks Hogwarts Legacy-inspired outfits, accessories, and more in the Portrait Maker feature.

    Should I link accounts in Hogwarts Legacy?

    Yes, there are perks to linking accounts in Hogwarts Legacy.     Can you change your house in Hogwarts Legacy if you linked your account?
