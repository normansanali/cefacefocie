    THE HIT singing competition show, The Voice, returned for its season 23 in March 2023.

    One of the fan-favorite groups that season was the sister trio, Sorellé.

    Who is The Voice trio Sorellé?

    The Voice, a competition show known for finding the next great singer, has continued through season 23 with large amounts of talent.

    One standout group, the trio Sorellé - which means sisters in Italian - caught fans' attention early on in the season.

    Sorellé consists of the Heichel sisters, Madi, Ana, and Bella, who hail from Lexington, Ohio.

    While they've gained attention from the world from their debut on The Voice, the Heichel sisters are seasoned performers.

    When Madi and Ana were around the ages of five to eight, they began vocal training.

    In 2012, Madi and Ana performed the National Anthem at a Cleveland Cavaliers game.

    They also appeared on Nickelodeon's America's Most Musical Family and recorded original music growing up.

    Their sister, Bella, recently joined in with her sisters to pursue a musical career.

    During their initial audition, Sorellé sang Queen's Good Old-Fashioned Lover Boy.

    The trio turned the chairs of coaches Blake Shelton and Chance the Rapper.

    Ultimately, the trio chose Chance.

    Did Sorellé win The Voice?

    On Tuesday, May 23, 2023, the 23rd season of The Voice came to an end when the final five contestants competed for the title.

    During the episode, competitor Gina Miles was declared the winner. Some fans were not pleased with the results of the finale, and claimed that the show 'is rigged.'

    How can I watch The Voice?

    The season finale of The Voice premiered on May 23, 2023, at 9pm ET on NBC.

    Fans of The Voice can continue to watch the show via streaming services.

    Previous episodes of season 23 of The Voice are available for streaming on Peacock, Hulu, YouTube TV, and FuboTV.

    This season is the final season with Blake Shelton.
