    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The December 27, 2023, episode of AEW Dynamite is a highly anticipated show and will follow up on the excellent December 20th edition of the show, as the Continental Classic tournament will be in full force for both the Gold and Blue leagues. It is also the go-home edition of the show before AEW Worlds End on December 30th.

    Tonight’s episode of AEW Dynamite will be taking place at the Additions Financial Arena in Orlando, Florida at 8 PM ET on TBS. Needless to say, fans can look forward to a lot of excellent in-ring action.

    The biggest draw for the show is undoubtedly the Continental Classic tournament, as AEW has loaded up Dynamite in a big way. The Gold League tournament matches feature Jon Moxley vs Swerve Strickland vs Jay White in the finals for the Gold League.

    In the Blue League finals, Bryan Danielson will be squaring off against his arch-nemesis Eddie Kingston. MJF and Samoa Joe continue their storyline with The Devil on tonight’s show. The AEW World Champion and Samoa Joe will defend the ROH Tag Team Championships against two unnamed henchmen of The Devil on the show. Renee Paquette will also interview Mariah May.

    AEW Dynamite December 27th, 2023 Lineup:

    • Jon Moxley vs. Jay White vs. Swerve Strickland– AEW Continental Classic Gold League match
    • Bryan Danielson vs. Eddie Kingston – AEW Continental Classic Blue League match
    • MJF & Samoa Joe vs. Two of The Devil’s henchmen
    • Renee Paquette will also interview Mariah May
    • The final buildup for AEW Worlds End
