    Helena Mattsson is a Swedish actress who works as one in Hollywood. After playing a minor role in the film Americanizing Shelley, she was cast for a starring role in Species: The Awakening. Before the 2010s ended, she had roles in the movies You and I and Surrogates. She also appeared as Rebecca in Iron Man 2. It was also around the same time that she had a recurring role on Desperate Housewives and then on Nikita. She then became part of the main cast of the short-lived series 666 Park Avenue. Her most recent work on TV were in Betrayal, Fargo, and American Horror Story: Hotel.
