    Who is Toby Keith?

    Toby Keith Covel, also known as Toby Keith, is a well-known figure in American country music. He is not only a singer but also a skilled songwriter, actor, and record producer. Born and raised in Clinton, Oklahoma, Toby was brought up by his parents Carolyn Joan and Hubert K. Covel, Jr., along with his siblings.

    During his early school years in Fort Smith, Arkansas, Toby’s family eventually settled in Moore, Oklahoma. It was during his visits to his grandmother’s establishment, Billie Garner’s Supper Club, that Toby’s passion for music was ignited. Starting with odd jobs at the club, he eventually joined the band on stage and received his first guitar at the young age of eight. While attending Highland West Junior High and Moore High School, Toby also displayed his athletic talent as a defensive end on the football team.

    After graduating from Moore High School, Toby pursued a career in the oil industry. He began as a derrick hand and steadily worked his way up to becoming an operations manager. Despite his professional commitments, Toby remained devoted to his musical aspirations.

    At the age of 20, he co-founded the Easy Money Band with his friends, including Scott Webb, Keith Cory, David “Yogi” Vowell, and Danny Smith. The band performed at local bars while Toby managed to balance his responsibilities in the oil field. There were even instances when he had to abruptly leave concerts if duty called in the middle of a show.
