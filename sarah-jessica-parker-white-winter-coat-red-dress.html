    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Sarah Jessica Parker made a stylish appearance at the Good+Foundation’s A Very Good+ Night of Comedy benefit at Midtown Manhattan’s Carnegie Hall on Wednesday.

    The Sex and the City star stepped out in a white double-breasted knee-length coat with jeweled buttons over a crimson dress that hit right above her ankles. She included another pop of color with bright magenta heels.

    The six-time Golden Globe winner accessorized with sparkling silver earrings and a silver crossbody bag. She completed the look with her signature heavy black eyeliner and glossy lips, and wore her blonde-highlighted waves down in a middle part.

    Jamie McCarthy//Getty Images

    She wasn’t the only one dressed in white. Comedian and actor Amy Schumer opted for a white mid-sleeve, V-neck midi dress and white strappy heels.

    Parker appears always to be channeling her inner Carrie Bradshaw when hitting the red carpet. The actor attended the New York City Ballet Fall Gala earlier this month looking ballerina-chic in a black tulle gown by Carolina Herrera that featured a long-sleeved off-the-shoulder bodice and a full-length skirt. Parker completed the look by pulling her hair back in a half-up, half-down do with a big black bow. She accessorized with similar sparkling silver Leighton Jewels jewelry and purse, as well as mismatched SJP heels.

    Parker plays the same onscreen fashionista that fans know and love in And Just Like That…, the SATC reboot, which boasted a top fashion budget of $3 million for season two. The show has been renewed for a third season.
