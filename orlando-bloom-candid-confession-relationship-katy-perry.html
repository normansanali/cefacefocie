    Orlando Bloom has shared a candid glimpse inside his relationship with fiance Katy Perry after postponing their wedding on two occasions. In a new interview with Flaunt magazine, the actor, 46, made a very frank admission regarding the couple's rollercoaster relationship.

    The couple got engaged in 2019

      Orlando Bloom has shared a candid glimpse inside his relationship with fiancée Katy Perry after postponing their wedding on two occasions.

      In a new interview with Flaunt magazine, the actor, 46, made a very frank admission regarding the couple's rollercoaster relationship.

      He went on to say: "Sometimes things are really, really, really, challenging. I won't lie. We definitely battle with our emotions and creativity."

      Katy and Orlando found love in 2016, with the star later popping the question in 2019. In August 2020, the loved-up duo welcomed their first child together – a daughter called Daisy Dove. Meanwhile Orlando is also a devoted father to son Flynn, whom he shares with his ex-wife, Miranda Kerr.
