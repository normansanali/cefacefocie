    I would call layering winter’s biggest hack, but it’s really more like the season’s biggest necessity. It’s the only thing that keeps us from freezing outdoors while preventing us from overheating once inside. We all have our go-to pieces — I’m a hoodie under a topcoat girl — but there’s one pairing that’s so sophisticated, both Kate Middleton and Meghan Markle repeatedly wear it. Earlier this week, Markle wore a fitted brown turtleneck top under the sleekest, milk chocolate-colored Valentino pant suit. The Duchess single-handedly took latte-dressing to new levels of chic.

    This styling has long been a go-to for Middleton, who has proven the pairing can be both sophisticated and playful with her own Barbie-inspired look. Now with not one but two royal stamps of approval, I’m fully convinced every winter wardrobe needs this elevated basic. So I went to Amazon and found eight spot-on lookalikes for Markle’s style, and prices start at just $15.

    8 Meghan Markle-Inspired Turtleneck Tops

    Herou Turtleneck Top

    Herou’s shopper-loved turtleneck top is now just $15, and the style is available in 28 colors, including a dark espresso — like Markle’s. With more than 6,300 five-star ratings, customers say the fabric is “soft, stretchy, and comfy for a long day [of] wear.” Another person called it “very flattering, soft, and comfy,” noting it’s one of the “most important pieces in [their] wardrobe.”

    ReoRia Cropped Turtleneck Top

    If you prefer to rock a cropped top with your favorite pair of high-waisted pants, ReoRia’s long-sleeve turtleneck top is an excellent option. According to one person, this is the “perfect winter basic” because it’s “incredibly soft,” while “the crop is [the] perfect length for jeans and leggings.” “This turtleneck fits like a glove,” said another shopper, who added that they “love that it’s a basic you can still dress up.”

    Mangopop Turtleneck Bodysuit

    Mangopop’s turtleneck bodysuit, which snaps closed at the bottom, is loved by more than 33,500 Amazon shoppers — many of whom rave about how “sexy” and “chic” the style is. “I love that this gives off a sleek polished look with jeans in the office, but easily changes over to dinner and drinks after,” said one person who added that it’s “big-bust- and plus-size-friendly.” “This bodysuit is fantastic,” wrote someone else who loved the “soft, stretchy fabric, easy snaps,” and how it gave them that chic, “tucked-in look without [having to] worry about it coming untucked.” According to another, the bodysuit is “a great staple piece to have in your wardrobe for layering winter outfits.”

    Turtleneck tops are the versatile staple Meghan Markle and Kate Middleton both reach for when in need of the perfect winter layer, so make sure to grab one from Amazon while the style starts at just $15.
