    As Sharon Cuneta’s leading man in Viva Films’ Sana’y Wala ng Wakas in 1986, he debuted and later signed an exclusive contract to appear in all of the Company’s movies.

    Tonton won Best Actor awards for the mentioned film from the Film Academy of the Philippines (FAP), PMPC’s Star Awards, and Catholic Mass Media Awards (CMMA) in 1987.

    He won the Gawad Urian Award for Best Actor in 1996 for Reyna Films’ Abot Kamay ang Pangarap.
