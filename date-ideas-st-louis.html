    Toasted ravioli is a St. Louis classic! This deep-fried delight was created in the city and is easy to come by in The Hill, St. Louis’ Italian neighborhood. If you and your date are already familiar with the dish, now it’s time to try and make your own!

    Rent an awesome Peerspace with a fabulous kitchen like this one, and get to cooking in the kitchen. The best part is — you don’t even have to clean up the house before your date comes over. Your Peerspace venue will be sparkling clean and ready for you when you arrive!

    Take it from a Peerspace reviewer who’s already spent time in this amazing loft: “This loft is breath taking! So gorgeous. Tons of natural light. The kitchen had everything you could need and more. Everything was clean and Jenna was accommodating”

    Forest Park is one of the largest urban parks in America and offers plenty of date ideas all in one spot! The park is home to the Missouri History Museum, Science Center, and the Steinberg Skating Rink. There is also Ant Hill, which is the perfect spot for a picnic with a view and you can rent a paddle boat to explore the waters after lunch! 

    If you are looking for date ideas in St. Louis before heading to the park, consider checking out the Soulard Farmers Market. It’s one of the oldest markets in the Midwest and with over 150 stalls, you are sure to find some amazing treats that you can share later, during your picnic in the park!

    Not only is the National Blues Museum wonderful to wander around as one of your date ideas in St. Louis— but you can also enjoy live music at the museum! Check out their website for a full calendar of events and jam sessions you can enjoy with your SO.

    There is no one who will be more excited to third-wheel on a date night than your dog! Invite Fido along to an awesome dog-friendly date at this stunning patio bar and restaurant in Soulard. This Peerspace includes an awesome dog-friendly patio space that you, your date, and your doggo will love. Especially since it has heating and air conditioning, keeping you comfortable year-round!

    The former home of Ulysses S. Grant is now owned by the Busch Family (ya, the beer-brewing ones!) and is home to more than 900 animals. Grant’s Farm features lemurs, kangaroos, buffalos, camels, ostriches— and of course, the Budweiser Clydesdales! It’s a super fun date idea St. Louis couples can enjoy — and it features some pretty cool history of the former president too!
