    Devoleena Bhattacharjee (born August 22, 1990) is an Indian TV Actress and Bharatanatyam Dancer from Sivsasagar, India. In fact, she is popular on TV for the role of “Gopi Bahu” in Saath Nibhana Saathiya. She paired opposite Mohammad Nazim. Both of them come together in “Laal Ishq” (2018) show on “&TV“.

    This show aired on Star Plus for more than seven years. Finally, it ended its journey in 2017. However, she entered in the catfight in 2015 with actress Loveleen Kaur Sasan.

    Loveleen entered in the show to play the role of Paridhi. After that, she fought with actor Vishal Singh too. On the other hand, she becomes a household as “Gopi”.

    She plays the role of a god-fearing daughter-in-law in the TV series. Meanwhile, she claims that she is leaving the show due to frequent leaps.
