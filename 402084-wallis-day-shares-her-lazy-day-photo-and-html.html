    17 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Wallis Day looked effortlessly stunning in a recent Instagram picture, which received thousands of likes and praise from fans. The "Sex Life" actress shot to fame following her role in the series, where she played a pregnant love interest and left fans wondering if she was really pregnant.

    On March 28, Day took to Instagram to share a picture of herself lying in bed in a pair of Kelvin Klein underwear and a white tank top. She sported a blonde pixie cut with her natural roots showing and captioned the post, "laazzzzy day."

    In the comments section, social media users could not believe how stunning the actress looked, with one person writing, "If only I could look this good on a lazy day. You should see me right now, I look like the trash."

    Day revealed that she was "v tired (sic)" to a user who noted she looked different, and they responded, "Yet, somehow managed to look like this !!!" Another person commented, "I wanted to send you a poem, but you are the most beautiful verse that life has ever written. Just an angel that God put in this world to shine."

    As a young girl, people always told Day she looked like a model. So one day, she did something about it and found an agency in London. By the time she was 15, she was hanging out with other models, sneaking into after-parties, and making it back to her parents' home during the early hours of the morning before she had to get up for school.

    Day's modeling career funded her studies at the Sylvia Young Theatre School and the Arts Educational Schools in London. However, acting ultimately won her heart. The actress said:

    "I fell in love with acting more because it allows so much freedom and movement in front of the screen, creating a character and backstory, bringing that to life. With modeling, it's somebody else's vision, someone else's creative decisions. Essentially I feel like a mannequin for a brand or client rather than I was creating."

    While Day's acting career has seen her star in multiple hit projects, her popularity skyrocketed after she landed the role of Gigi, Adam Demos' on-screen love interest in "Sex Life." Day's character is pregnant in the show's second season, and her hyper-realistic baby bump left fans wondering if the actress was pregnant in real life. However, she set the record straight and said, "That wasn't my real belly. They're prosthetics."
