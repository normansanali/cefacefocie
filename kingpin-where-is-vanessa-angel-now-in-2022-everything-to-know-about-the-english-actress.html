    Vanessa Angel is an excellent English actress and former model, known for her role of Claudia in the movie Kingpin, who is recently busy with her movie project ‘Trouble Sleeping.’

    The former Fort model Vanessa Angel gained recognition after portraying the character of Lisa on the television series Weird Science (1994-1998) and Claudia in the 1996 classic Farrelly brothers’ comedic movie Kingpin.

    The English actress started her professional career as an actor in 1985 with the American comedy movie Spies Like Us, where she played the role of a soldier in the Soviet Strategic Missile Forces.

    Where Is Vanessa Angel Now In 2022?

    The actress and former model Vanessa Angel is busy with her movie projects. On February 15, 2022, the thriller movie ‘Trouble Sleeping’ got released in the USA, where Vanessa played the role of lead character ‘Vanessa’ and Robert Adetuyi directed and wrote the movie.

    Vanessa gained popularity from the television series Weird Science as Lisa; the computer-generated genie role originated by Kelly LeBrock in the 1985 movie upon which the series got based.

    Vanessa played the role of Natasha in the 1998 comedy movie Kissing a Fool alongside David Schwimmer and as a Debra in the 1999 movie Made Men. In 2014, the actress appeared in various films, including Superbabies: Baby Geniuses 2, The Perfect Score, and Out for Blood.

    The former model did the independent movie Blind Ambition in 2007, where she got seen as Autumn Milestone. The same year she did a television film, Planet Raptor, and portrayed the character of Dr. Anna Rogers.

    The actress Vanessa Angel was born in London, England, on November 10, 1966, and is currently 55 years old. Her birth name is Vanessa Madeline Angle.

    She was born in a well-off English family toPeterAngle and Elizabeth Angle. Her father, Peter Angle, was a stockbroker in England, and her mother was a homemaker. She has a sibling, Mayang Sary, and holds British citizenship.

    The actress had a beautiful face and body, so from an early age gained attention. Because of this, many model-management companies tried to hire her.

    Eventually, the London-born actor got first scouted at a cafe when she was just a teenager, and New York’s giant modeling agency Ford Models signed her at the young age of 16.

    After entering into the fashion industry, she got inclined towards acting and got her first project in 1985, where she played a soldier in the Soviet Strategic Missile Forces. After her debut, it took three years to get another project.

    Vanessa made her television debut with the series ‘The Equalizer,’ where she has a guest spot as Christine Hayes in the episode ‘Video Games.’ She played the recurring character of Megan on Baywatch and Reasonable Doubts as Detective Peggy Elliott.
