    Here's the good news: David Beckham is going to make an appearance in the upcoming film King Arthur: Legend of the Sword. The bad news: you probably won't be able to point him out.

    The soccer star is headed to the big screen to play a knight in the Guy Ritchie-directed epic drama, which stars Charlie Hunnam and Jude Law. (Beckham perviously previously made an appearance in Ritchie's 2015 film, The Man from U.N.C.L.E.)

    The father of four shared a behind-the-scenes photo in full makeup, and it's a pretty gruesome look:

    "Rough day at the office," he captioned the shot on Instagram, showing off his faux scars, wounds, bald spots and dirtied teeth. Rough indeed.

    King Arthur: Legend of the Sword hits theaters on May 17. Watch the trailer below.
