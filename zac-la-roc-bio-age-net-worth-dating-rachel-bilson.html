    In case you’re interested, Leticia’s a glass artist whose work was featured on the cover for the 2019 issue of DTLA Book — an annual hardcover yearbook all about the people and places who make up downtown Los Angeles.

    Now coming back to Zac’s career, he isn’t just an artist. This art lover has also dabbled in the movie industry. And when yet another love affair is discovered, all three women team up to plot revenge on their “boyfriend.”
