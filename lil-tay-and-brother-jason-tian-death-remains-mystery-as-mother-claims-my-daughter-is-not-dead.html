    Confusion has engulfed the online community as conflicting statements emerge about the reported death of 14-year-old Canadian influencer, Lil Tay. What was initially announced as a “sudden and tragic passing” has been cast into doubt, with her father and ex-manager failing to confirm the tragic news.

    In a mysterious turn of events, Lil Tay’s mother, Angela Tian, took to her Instagram account @iamangelatian to assert, “My daughter is not dead, her father Christopher Hope needs to be permanently removed From all visitation With My Child I Have Her & we would’ve been closing our case this coming Sunday with all rights and this is the outcome we get Do not believe this at all this is 100% incorrect information – Angela Tian.”

    She further expressed her frustration with her daughter’s father, Christopher Hope, and demanded that he be removed from visitation rights.
