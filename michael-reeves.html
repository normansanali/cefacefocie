    Wiki/Biography

    Michael Reeves was born on Thursday, November 20, 1997 (age 23 years; as in 2020) in Los Angeles, California. His zodiac sign is Scorpio. He did a BS (Bachelor of Science) in Computer Science from the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa.

    Physical Appearance

    Height (approx.): 5′ 6″

    Eye Color: Dark Brown

    Hair Color: Dark Brown

    Family, Ethnicity & Relationships

    Not much is known about his family. In February 2020, it was announced that he was dating LilyPichu, a Korean American musician, twitch streamer, and YouTuber.

    Michael Reeves with LilyPichu

    Career

    In 2016, he began working as a software developer at the University of Hawaiʻi Maui College (UHMC) for eight months. He then joined Nothern Arizona University Kjack Radio as the head of software development and worked there for eight months. He then joined the UHMC Agricultural Department as a software developer. He worked for almost three and a half years as a Software Developer, IT, Client Acquisition/Relations, Financial Manager at INFIBIT LLC. In 2017, he worked at UMHC-OCET as a software developer. He uploaded his first YouTube video ‘The Robot That Shines a Laser in Your Eye’ posted in 2017, which went viral. He has more than 4.1M subscribers on his YouTube Channel.

    In June 2020, he joined Twitch; there he streams both video games and technology work. In December 2019, he was officially announced as a member of OfflineTV, an online social entertainment group of content creators based in Los Angeles, California.

    Michael Reeves with a Member of Offline TV

    Facts/Trivia

    • He was the winner of the Maui STEM Conference Cyber Security Competition by MEDB, Women in Technology Team in May 2016.
    • In August 2016, he won Performance Based Monetary Award by UHMC, Rural Hawaii Grant Project for exemplary software development on a critical project for the department.
    • He is an animal lover and is often seen helping animals or posing with them on his social media accounts.
