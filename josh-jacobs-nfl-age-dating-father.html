    Josh Jacobs is an American football running back, currently playing for the Oakland Raiders (now called Las Vegas Raiders) of the National Football League (NFL). Previously, he played college football at the University of Alabama.

    Josh Jacobs Bio; Age, Family, Education, Height, Weight

    Joshua Jacobs was born on 11 February 1988 as one of four children of Marty Jacobs and Lachelle Jacobs. He has three brothers and a sister named Shaleya Jacobs. His younger brother, Isaiah Jacobs, is a runningback for the Maryland Terrapins. 

    Josh Jacobs in a childhood picture with his siblings (Picture: Jacobs’ Instagram)

    The 22-years-of-age player pursued his early education at McLain High School in Tulsa, Oklahoma. He then went to the University of Alabama to play college football. He has a muscular body with a weight of 5 feet 10 inches (1.78 meters) and weighs 220 lbs (100 kg).

    His Career NFL, Stats

    Josh played for the Alabama Crimson Tide for three seasons from 2016 to 2018. He had 284 yards on 46 carries with one touchdown in the 2017 season.

    Josh Jacobs NFL career stats as of 2020 (Picture: ESPN)

    Jacobs was selected by the Oakland Raiders (now Las Vegas Raiders) in the first round (24th overall) of the 2019 NFL Draft. He made his NFL debut in Week 1 game against the Denver Broncos on 10 September 2019. In the 24-16 victory, he rushed 23 times for 85 yards and two touchdowns and caught one pass for 28 yards. He had impressive stats of 1,150 rushing yards and seven rushing touchdowns in the 2019 season.

    As of the current 2020 season, Josh’s stats read at 9 touchdowns for 782 rushing yards from 11 games.
