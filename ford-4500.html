    Ford 4500 Industrial Tractor is equipped with a Ford diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 56.5 hp (42.1 kW) power at a rated rpm of 2200.

    Ford 4500 Industrial Tractor featured a Ford 3.3L 3-cyl diesel and Ford 3.1L 3-cyl gasoline engine, 4×2 2WD, power assist steering, mechanical wet disc brakes, 2000 lbs rear lift, 4510 to 7233 pounds weight, 6 forward and 4 reverse gears, 13.8 Qts 13.1 L oil capacity.

    Ford 4500 Industrial tractor attachments include Ford 740 front-end loader and Ford 753, Ford 755 backhoe, and Ford Side-Shift Backhoe.
