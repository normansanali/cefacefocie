    If Kim Kardashian knows anything, it's how to make an entrance.

    Yesterday, the fashion mogul headed to the stylish New York City neighborhood of SoHo, en route to her guest appearance on Jimmy Fallon's The Tonight Show. Of course, she didn't arrive without the paparazzi snapping a few photos of her, and what they captured is sure to come down in history as one of Ms. Kardashian's most iconic looks: skin-tight Helmut Lang leather pants, black wedged Yeezy sandals, and the sheerest-ever halter top by Church Boutique.

    And for those worried that Kim's free-the-nipple top would compromise Fallon's PG rating, don't fret. It looks like the piece wasn't nearly as see-through once the cameras started rolling.

    NBC//Getty Images

    During the show, Kardashian also addressed the rumors that her and husband Kanye West were leaving Los Angeles after they purchased a ranch in Wyoming. While they enjoy the Wild West state, it doesn't look like the Kardashian-West clan will be uprooting any time soon.

    “We love Wyoming, it’s always been such an amazing place,” she said. “My husband did just buy a ranch there, and his dream and his vision is to move there [...] I love LA, so I envision summers, I envision some weekends. But yeah, we love it.”
