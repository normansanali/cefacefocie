    Who is Ian Ziering?

    Ian Ziering is an American actor and voice artist. He is most popular for writing such as Beverly Hills, 90210, Biker Mice from Mars, Swamp Thing, and more.

    Moreover, his upcoming project is an action titled Surviving Pacifica which is in the post-production stage.

    Ian Ziering- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

    He was born Ian Andrew Ziering on 30 March 1964 in Newark, New Jersey, USA. The name of his mother and his father was Muriel Ziering and Paul M. Ziering respectively.

    Ian’s ancestry is Ashkenazi-Jewish and has two older brothers named Jeff & Barry.

    About academics, he first attended West Orange High School and passed out in 1982. Then he completed his studies at William Paterson University in 1988.

    Ian Ziering- Professional Life, Career

    Ian Ziering is mostly known for his TV roles. He had brief roles in The Doctors, ABC Afterschool Specials, Married…with Children, JAG, Battle of the Network Stars, and more.

    He was also seen dancing in Dancing With The Stars in 2007 with his partner Cheryl Burke. Fortunately, he reached the semi-finals but lost the competition. Ian also competed in Celebrity Name Game, The Apprentice, Worst Cooks in America, and more.
