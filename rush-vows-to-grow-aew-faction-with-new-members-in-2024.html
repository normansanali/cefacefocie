    RUSH Vows to Grow AEW Faction with New Members in 2024

    22 Sep 2024, 00:00

    All Elite Wrestling has one of the deepest rosters in all of professional wrestling, whether that may be the men’s roster or the women’s roster and one of the most athletic and popular stars in the men’s division is “El Toro Blanco” RUSH.

    RUSH is the leader of the La Faccion Ingobernable faction, with Jose The Assistant, Preston Vance and Dralistico as its members, but despite the group’s dominance in the company over the past several months, RUSH is still looking to add more members this year, which he revealed on Twitter (X), one of which is a man and the other member will be a woman.

    “2024 will see a bigger LA FACCION INGOBERNABLE

    I will add a new Man to the group

    And a WOMAN.

    NO PASA NADA”

    La Faccion Ingobernable’s newest members could come from a number of different places like CMLL, NJPW or even from AEW itself as the company has had a working relationship with several promotions for quite some time now. The group continues to run roughshod each and every time they are on television and they will continue to do so, especially with new members on the horizon.
