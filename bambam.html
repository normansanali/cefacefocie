    Who exactly is BamBam?

    Kunpimook Bhuwakul, better known as BamBam, is a Thai singer, rapper, dancer, songwriter, and record producer based in South Korea. He is best known for being a member of the South Korean band ‘Got7,’ along with Junior, JB, Jackson, Yu-gym, Mark, and Young-Jae. To date, this band has issued two box sets, three studio albums, ten extended plays, and thirteen singles. Bhuwakul, who was named after Bamm-Bamm Rubble from the popular cartoon show “The Flintstones,” has also appeared in several films and variety shows as an actor and dancer. He has also given incredible live performances across the country as a singer and rapper. He and his band members received the 2015 Golden Disk Award and the Seoul Music Award. He is a very ambitious person who has set lofty goals for himself. Despite his youth, he is highly disciplined in his career and does not waste his time on frivolous pursuits. Bhuwakul, on the other hand, is averse to the media. He dislikes being in the spotlight for reasons other than his professional achievements.

    BamBam Career

    Before joining Got7, Bhuwakul trained for three and a half years with JYP Entertainment. His first pre-debut appearance was as Wong Wai-son Han’s in the film ‘Fairy Tale Killer.’ He later appeared in an episode of the reality show WIN: Who Is Next, which aired in September 2013. The following year, Bhuwakul’s band Got7 released their debut album ‘Identity,’ as well as two EPs titled ‘Got It’ and ‘Got Love.’ This was followed by two additional EPs, titled ‘Just Right’ and ‘Mad.’
    Bhuwakul was named the permanent host of Mnet’s ‘M! Countdown’ in 2015. He also appeared in the Korean-Chinese web drama ‘Dream Knight’ that year. A year later, the Thai actor/singer appeared on the variety show “Real Men” as well as on KBS’ “Idol Battle Likes.” The same year, he made a cameo appearance on SBS’s “Don’t Dare to Dream.” That year, he released two studio albums with the band, titled ‘Flight Log: Turbulence’ and ‘Moriagatteyo.’
    Bhuwakul became a cast member of ‘3 zaps’ in 2017. His band Got7 released their EPs ‘Flight Log: Arrival’ and ‘7 for 7’ the same year. Then, on March 12, 2018, they released their second EP, ‘Eyes on You,’ which included the hit single “One and Only You.” Bhuwakul returned to Thailand for a military draft on April 9, 2018.

    Personal Existence

    Kunpimook Kochakorn Bhuwakul and her husband gave birth to Bhuwakul on May 2, 1997, in Bangkok, Thailand. Bhuwakul’s father died when he was a child. He practices Buddhism. He has one younger sister and two older brothers who are all well-known dancers. He went to Pramoch Wittaya Ramindra School and won the ‘Rain Cover’ dance competition when he was ten years old. He is fluent in Thai, English, and Korean. Bhuwakul currently owns three dogs.
