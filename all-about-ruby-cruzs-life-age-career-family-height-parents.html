    Career

    Ruby Cruz has had a remarkable career with her impressive appearances in many television shows and series. 2018 saw her in the role of the younger Annie Wilkes, played by Lizzy Caplan in the famed TV show Castle Rock.

    TVLine lauded her for her ability to bring out the body language of Caplan’s performance, displaying her meticulousness and talents as an actress. In 2020, Cruz was featured in the tenth season of the highly praised TV show Blue Bloods, showing her flexibility in her craft.

    At the start of 2021, Cruz portrayed Jess Riley in the HBO drama series Mare of Easttown, thereby increasing her body of work with an impressive performance.

    In March of 2021, Cruz’s career hit a new high when she was cast as Kit Tanthalos, the lead character, in the highly-anticipated Disney+ series Willow. Making Cruz’s selection even more remarkable is the fact that Kit is the first lesbian protagonist in a Disney production, which is a groundbreaking event in the entertainment industry.

    The 2022 Willow TV series serves as an extension to the 1988 movie with the same title, thus increasing Cruz’s fame as an actor.

    Apart from her TV work, Cruz made her big-screen bow as Hazel Callahan in the forthcoming movie Bottoms, set to come out in 2023. With her skill and determination, she is still making an impact on TV and in the cinema.
