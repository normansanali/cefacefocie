    The long-running The Fast and the Furious franchise’s ninth instalment, F9, opened in theatres to a lot of Twitter excitement. Dominic “Dom” Toretto, played by Vin Diesel, and his motley family have long been the subject of fan speculation. However, compared to other earlier films in the series, this most recent entry received a different kind of reception.

    As soon as F9 was released in North American theatres, a plethora of memes honouring Dom and his devotion to his family appeared on Twitter. In other memes, Dom even crosses over into other fandoms to emphasise the value of family, which often results in absurd events.

    While Groot was present for the final showdown with Thanos in Avengers: Endgame, some fans speculate what may have happened if Thanos had been killed by a different Vin Diesel character.     Tony Stark gave his life to utilise the Infinity Gauntlet, which is too strong for ordinary humans to use against Thanos. But Dom Toretto’s faith in the strength of his family is so great that it just could give him the willpower he needs to outlive even death.
