    Ryan Charles- Contestant From The American Song Contest

    26 Sep 2024, 00:00

    On Monday, March 21, NBC premiered American Song Contest, a new music competition series featuring 56 musicians. Ryan Charles, a great rapper who represents his homeland of Wyoming, is one of the candidates.

    Charles grew up in Buffalo, Wyoming, and now lives in Nashville, Tennessee. He is recognized for his distinct sense of style when it comes to adopting the cowboy look. It’s become his distinctive look, and it’s been seen in nearly all of his music videos.

    The western rapper is characterized as follows in his bio on his website:

    “Similar to his birthplace, which was previously known as America’s most lawless town, Charles has his unique style, fusing the cowboy lifestyle with the flow of 2000s rap.”

    His Instagram account is full of photos of him rocking the cowboy look at various events and locations.

    Ryan Charles Musical Career

    Ryan Charles discovered his love for music from a young age. His description described him as a “natural-born entertainer” who “soon developed into a freestyle rapper” and opted to follow his career in Seattle, Washington.

    He moved to Nashville and continued to perform “light-hearted twang and high-energy live concerts.” Charles’ debut song, Gettin’ Western, was released in 2020, followed by Old Dirt Fancy in 2021. He was a featured musician on Ian Munsick’s track Cowboy Killer as of 2022.

    According to his Instagram account, the rapper enjoys a genuine cowboy lifestyle. On his social media, he has a number of images of himself in camouflaged costumes. He also likes hunting with his companions while dressed in such costumes. In his Instagram photographs, Charles may also be spotted golfing, fishing, and snowboarding.

    Charles was praised for his performance at the American Song Contest.

    Charles’ performance in the American Song Contest has gotten a lot of positive feedback online. The Wyoming native performed his new track, New Boot Goofin’, in his trademark way. Through the choreography of his performance on this tune, he offered the audience a taste of the cowboy lifestyle.

    The NBC show is a competition series in which contestants compete for the title of “Best Original Song.” All 50 U.S. states, five U.S. territories, and the nation’s capital, Washington, D.C., are represented. They’ll play live original music, and fans may vote for their favorite contestant on the NBC website, app, and TikTok. American Song Contest, hosted by Snoop Dogg and Kelly Clarkson, will premiere new episodes every Monday at 8 p.m. ET on NBC.
