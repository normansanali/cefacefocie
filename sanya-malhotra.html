    Career Path

    After her graduation she moved to Mumbai to start her career in dancing. For which she has appeared in dancing reality show Dance India Dance but could not come upto top 100. 

    After that she started giving auditions for TV commercials and series, she appeared in so many commercials before starting her Film career. 

    In 2016, She was selected for the role of one of the Phogat Sisters. She worked along with Aamir khan, Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sakshi Tanwar, Aparshakti Khurana, and Zaira Wasim. 

    Recently she has appeared with Vidya Balan Starrer a biopic movie Shakuntala Devi (2020). In 2021, she played a role in Movie Ludo along with Abhishek Bachchan, Aditya Roy Kapur, Pankaj Tripathi, Rajkumar Rao and Fatima Sana Shiekh. 

    Now her new Movie named Pagglait has been released online and can be seen on Netflix.

    Personal Life of Sanya Malhotra

    Sanya was born in Delhi on 25th February 1992 in a middle-class family. Her father is a Delhi-based Engineer and her mother is a homemaker. She has one elder sister. 

    She did her schooling at Ryan International School, Delhi, and completed her graduation from Gargi College, University of Delhi. In her academics, She was a bright student. 

    From Childhood she had enthusiasm for dancing, for which she took training and she does ballet dancing also.
