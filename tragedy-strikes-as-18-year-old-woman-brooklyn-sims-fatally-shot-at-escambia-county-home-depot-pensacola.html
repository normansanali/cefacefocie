    ESCAMBIA COUNTY, Pensacola — A devastating incident unfolded at an Escambia County Home Depot, where a young 18-year-old woman lost her life in a shocking act of violence. The victim, identified as Brooklyn Sims, was tragically shot and killed on Friday while she was at work as a contracted inventory employee at the Home Depot located on Davis Highway.

    Brooklyn, a mother to a 2-year-old girl, was well-liked by friends and coworkers. The community is mourning the loss of this young life that was filled with promise and potential.

    The alleged perpetrator, Keith Agee, 20, has been taken into custody for the murder. Law enforcement officials revealed that Agee and Sims were acquainted with each other prior to the tragic incident. The motive behind the shooting remains under investigation.

    Disturbing text messages exchanged between Keith Agee and his mother, Sheila Agee, have come to light. The messages suggest a sinister plot leading up to the fatal shooting. According to authorities, Sheila Agee is now facing charges as an accessory to first-degree murder for her alleged role in coordinating the crime.
